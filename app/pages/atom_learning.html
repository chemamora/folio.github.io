Atom Learning

As the sole designer at the helm, my role has spanned a wide spectrum of domains within the business, affording me the insights necessary to cultivate both our brand's visual language and the cohesion of our design team.

In the initial phases of brand development, our primary focal point was the construction of a modular and user-friendly brand system. The objective was to craft a system that could be swiftly grasped by junior designers, all the while effectively embodying our company's core values.

Our fcommitment to modularity and accessibility remained central throughout the developmental process. Every facet of the brand was thoughtfully designed to facilitate ease of use for a wide array of in-house and external creatives, ensuring consistent brand representation across all media channels

Logo

The logo boasts a minimalist structure inspired by the iconic shapes found in Tangram games. It's both colorful and immediately eye-catching, helping us craft a sophisticated visual language that resonates with our user base and industry.

It employs a clever color-coding system to distinguish and identify different company products. The multi-colored version is strategically aimed at engaging students, while the flat version caters more to educational institutions. This dual approach ensures our logo remains versatile and relevant across various facets of our business.

Color & Type

Colour plays a critical role in shaping the brand personality as well as aids accessibility and product recognition. The colour combination is important and a detailed guide has been developed to help new designers to use it successfully.

As a direct reflection of the brand concept, the type system conveys the binary notion to express, fun and function.

Design System

Upon joining Atom Learning as the sole designer, I immediately noticed the lack of consistency between the designs and the actual codebase. It became clear that building a robust design system, assembling a dedicated design team, and establishing strong connections with the development team were essential steps to address this issue.

The primary challenge we faced was the absence of a unified approach to designing and building the platform. Thankfully, the front-end development team had already initiated discussions on how to resolve this issue. To expedite the process and foster a collaborative work environment, we formed a dedicated team with experts from both design and development. This dual-pronged approach allowed us to save time and ensured that both teams felt engaged and motivated to contribute to the development of the system.

Following several months of in-depth research, which involved gathering insights from current users and key stakeholders, we initiated the first phase of our project. This phase focused on enhancing the most frequently used and visited areas of the app. Simultaneously, we created the most commonly used components and design patterns. We established a structured work pipeline for subsequent sprints, enabling us to make continuous improvements and maintain a consistent design and development process.
