Hayter & Olivares Identity

The identity is modern and sophisticated, to align with the studio ethos, emphasizing the use of clean lines and earthy tones to reflect precision and natural materials.

The ampersand symbol plays a pivotal role in the brand. It represents the partnership between the two founders, underlining the collaborative spirit and shared commitment to the company's values and craft. This ampersand is used as a graphic element in various brand applications.

A complete set of collaterals has been crafted to maintain a consistent and cohesive brand language. This includes business cards, invoices, and other essential documents. Each collateral item is designed with a keen attention to detail, showcasing the company's commitment to quality and excellence.

The resulting brand identity creates a harmonious blend of modernity and tradition, emphasizing the company's core values, partnership, and the superior craftsmanship they offer to their clients.
