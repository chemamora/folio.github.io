Evolving Luno's Brand Language

Over the first year at Luno, I had the opportunity to develop and redefine our brand language and the way it was used across a multidisciplinary team of designers. This journey not only uncovered the nuances of our creative process but also highlighted the challenges we faced in achieving a consistent and cohesive brand identity.

In the initial stages of our research, it became evident that each designer on our team was interpreting and using the brand system in their unique way. This diversity in approach led to several challenges.

The inconsistency in applying the brand language across various assets made it difficult for the brand to be recognized consistently from one medium to another. Designers were using colors in a random manner, leading to confusion among our audience and a diluted brand identity. The absence of a unified message made it challenging to convey a clear and memorable brand narrative. Addressing these challenges, our team embarked on a mission to create a more cohesive and unified brand system.

We recognized the need for a consistent and easy-to-understand framework that could be embraced by every designer

Stablishing the work enviroment

We established clear design guidelines to standardise the use of color, typography, imagery, and space, ensuring a harmonious visual identity.

Invested time in training and workshops to ensure every team member understood and could effectively use the brand system. By developing a library of reusable brand assets, we made it simpler for designers to access and incorporate the elements into their work.

The efforts to redevelop our brand language started to yield positive results right away, resulting in the increase of media following count and excellent response from current users and an increase in new acquisitions. Our brand became more recognizable, and we conveyed a consistent message across various media. However, our journey continued, as we strived to adapt to changing design trends and ensure the brand's longevity.

In the fast-paced world of branding and design, maintaining a consistent and cohesive brand language was crucial. Through our journey at Luno, we learned that a unified brand system not only fostered recognition but also built trust with our audience. As we continued to evolve and adapt, we were confident that our efforts would strengthen our brand identity and make Luno even more impactful in the world of finance and technology.
