In this project we reimagined MABM identity and collateral graphic design work, we embarked on a transformative journey for the company's branding. This included a fresh approach to color combinations, ushering in a more minimalist aesthetic to align with contemporary design trends.

Working in close collaboration with the agency's art director, we created a comprehensive suite of cutting-edge print and digital designs for the company's flagship annual event. These designs embodied a modern and sleek visual language, resonating with the forward-looking direction of the company.

Our aim was to elevate the company's brand presence and create a lasting impact with a refined, minimalistic style. This new approach not only spoke to the present but also laid the foundation for a dynamic and compelling future for the company and its events.
